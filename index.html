Welcome to my profile

Currently I'm studying at RWTH Aachen, Germany. Since 2013 I am attending Computational Engineering Science classes at the university. In the next semesters I want to specialize in fluid dynamics possibly with the application in aerospace engineering. Apart from foundations of mechanical engineering I learned a lot about programming with C++ and Qt during my studies and assignments. In my spare time I love to travel and take photos or work on personal coding projects and I enjoy video games.

Projects at university

During my studies I worked on some projects either in a team or alone. In the following years a big group assignment, an internship as well as my bachelor thesis will contribute to this list.

  • 2015: TrussSimulator - As part of my degree students have to work in a team to create a small application with a background in engineering. The goal is to get familiar with all stages of software development from design to programming to testing. Our task was to create an easy to use program that allows the user to draw a two-dimensional truss and computes its deformation under load. We wrote the code in C++ with usage of the Qt library and Sundials Kinsol as a nonlinear solver. Unfortunately we are not allowed to make our repository public due to the exercise character of the project.
  • 2015-now: IFAS valveplate/cylinderblock project - As part of a project examining losses in hydraulic pumps at IFAS, I work on the implementation of a model for the behavior of a major bearing contact. My tasks include simplification of physical effects for the simulation and coding of the mathematical backend as well as the Qt interface. The most recent paper of the project was published at IFK 2016.
